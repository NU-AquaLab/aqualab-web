Kedar presented **“The Aleph: Decoding Geographic Information from DNS PTR Records Using Large Language Models”**
(*Kedar Thiagarajan, Esteban Carisimo, Fabián E. Bustamante*) at **CoNEXT '25** in Hong Kong.

The Aleph introduces a new approach to geolocation based on DNS PTR records; it uses large LLMs to extract geographic signals from noisy reverse DNS naming conventions and generate structured mappings deployable at scale.
